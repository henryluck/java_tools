1:Move about the stage using the arrow keys. Don't fret about moving offscreen.
2:Run over mushrooms to collect them, they'll follow behind you.
3:Once you've collected three mushrooms it's time to head back.
4:There are four types of mushrooms in all. Each worth more than the last.
5:Once you've got some mushrooms return to your picnic to collect them.
6:Try to avoid running in the sunlight though, you're Irish.
7:When in the sun your meter drains, when it's empty you lose.
8:You're mushrooms are Irish too, it seems. Try to keep them in the shade as well.
9:If all the action makes you giddy press P to pause the game.
10:Collect as many mushrooms as you can before nightfall. Good luck!
